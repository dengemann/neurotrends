Welcome to NeuroTrends!

NeuroTrends is a platform for organizing information about methods used in brain imaging research. NeuroTrends includes information on {{ narts }} published studies and {{ nattribs }} different design and analysis strategies. You can look up the design strategies, software packages, and statistical corrections used in a specific article. Or you can look up articles that used certain tools--for example, all the articles that used an event-related design analyzed using SPM with false discovery rate correction.

To get started, run a search or view the gallery. To access NeuroTrends from PubMed, drag the NeuroTrends Lookup bookmarklet to your browser links bar. When viewing an article in PubMed, click on the bookmarklet to see methods information in NeuroTrends.
